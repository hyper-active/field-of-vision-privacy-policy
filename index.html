Privacy Policy - Field Of Vision

We at Field Of Vision Creative LTD. (“Field Of Vision”, “we”, or “our”) value your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information from users of our games and services (“Services”), and how users can manage or delete their data.

Information We Collect

When you use our Services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data about how the game is used, device type, system performance, crash logs, and gameplay metrics. This helps us improve our games.
  • User ID and Username: If you connect to leaderboards or social features, your platform username and user ID may be processed to display rankings and scores. We do not collect email addresses or other identifying data unless you contact support.
  • Support Requests: If you contact our support team, we will collect the email address and any information you provide. This is only used to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use the Data

We use collected data to:

  • Monitor game performance and fix bugs.
  • Improve gameplay experience.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Enable platform features such as leaderboards.

Third-Party Services

  • Unity Analytics: We use Unity Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay metrics. See their policy: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Oculus / Meta: When using our games on the Meta Quest platform, Meta may collect personal information per their policy: https://www.oculus.com/legal/privacy-policy/

We only receive aggregate data from these services. We do not access or store individual user data beyond what is described here.

Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete your personal data (e.g., support emails or leaderboard username), you may request this by contacting us at support@fieldof.vision. We will confirm deletion within 30 days of verifying your request.

Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use commercially reasonable methods to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
